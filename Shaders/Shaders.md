
With OpenGL shaders are written in [GLSL (OpenGL Shading Language)](GLSL%20(OpenGL%20Shading%20Language).md). These shaders are part of the rendering pipeline and used to process raw data to actually render something on the screen.

![[pipeline.png]]
The most common and shaders are the [Vertex Shader](Vertex%20Shader.md) and [Fragment Shader](Fragment%20Shader.md) shaders used to set vertices and color respectively.
